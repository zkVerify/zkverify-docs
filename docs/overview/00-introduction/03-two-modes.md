---
title: "0.3 Two Modes (Critical to Understand From the Start)"
sidebar_position: 4
---

This section clearly distinguishes the two core usage modes of zkVerify to prevent confusion early on:

- Verify-only
  You only need the proof to be verified on zkVerify.
  The verification result is consumed in Web2 systems or other off-chain logic.
- Verify + Aggregate
  You need the verification result to be consumed by another chain or a smart contract, such as vFlow or an EVM-based chain.
