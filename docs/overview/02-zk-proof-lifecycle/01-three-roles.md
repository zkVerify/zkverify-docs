---
title: "2.1 The Three Roles (Explicit and Fixed)"
sidebar_position: 2
---

This section clearly defines the three roles that always exist in a ZK proof lifecycle:

- Producer (Project / Circuit Author)
  Responsible for writing the circuit or program.
- Prover (User / Client)
  Provides private inputs and generates the proof.
- Verifier (zkVerify / On-chain / Backend)
  Verifies whether the proof is valid.
